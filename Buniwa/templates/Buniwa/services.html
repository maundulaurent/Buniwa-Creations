Location-Based Marketing leverages mobile device location data to deliver targeted marketing messages to users based on their physical location. It's a powerful tool for brick-and-mortar businesses and service providers with a local focus.
In today's mobile-first world, reaching your target audience where they are is crucial for success. Buniwa Creations offers a suite of location-based marketing (LBM) services designed to put your brand directly in front of potential customers at the precise moment they need you. Here are some key LBM techniques Buniwa Creations can utilize for your business:

  • Geomarketing/Geofencing: We create virtual perimeters (geofences) around specific locations, such as your store or competitor locations. When a mobile user with location services enabled enters or exits the geofence, they receive targeted notifications or messages on their phones.
  • Mobile Marketing: We craft engaging SMS marketing campaigns or utilize push notifications within mobile apps to reach your target audience directly on their devices.
  • Beaconing: We integrate beacon technology with your marketing strategy. Beacons are small devices that transmit signals to nearby smartphones, allowing us to deliver hyper-targeted messages and promotions to customers within your store or at specific events.
  • Geotargeting: We leverage location data to tailor your online advertising campaigns to specific regions, cities, or even neighborhoods. This ensures your ads reach the most relevant audience, maximizing your return on investment (ROI).

Why It's Important: Increased Brand Awareness: Reach potential customers who are actively searching for products or services in your vicinity, driving brand awareness and foot traffic.
Enhanced Customer Engagement: Deliver targeted messages and promotions that are relevant to the user's location and needs, leading to higher engagement rates.
Personalized Marketing: Cater your marketing efforts to specific locations, allowing you to tailor offers and promotions based on local preferences.
Measurable Results: Track the effectiveness of your LBM campaigns with key metrics like click-through rates, conversion rates, and in-store visits to measure ROI.
Our Process: We work closely with you to understand your target audience and business goals. We then develop a customized LBM strategy that utilizes the most effective techniques for your specific needs.
  • Conduct thorough location-based market research.
  • Analyze competitor LBM strategies.
  • Identify the most relevant LBM techniques for your business.
  • Create targeted messaging and promotions for each location.
  • Implement and manage your LBM campaigns seamlessly.
  • Track and analyze campaign performance to ensure continuous optimization.
